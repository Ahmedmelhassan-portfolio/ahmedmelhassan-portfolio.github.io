General Notes

SCALE: N.T.S.

I am a civil engineer from Sudan, trained at Karadeniz Technical University in Trabzon, Türkiye. My education covered the full breadth of civil engineering — structural engineering, geotechnics, transportation, hydraulics and water resources, construction materials, surveying, and construction and project management — giving me a well-rounded foundation for any branch of the profession.

My graduation thesis focused on the design of a next-generation sustainable school campus, bringing structural design and sustainable building principles together in one project. Alongside engineering, I build tools for the engineering community — a Telegram bot that delivers lecture notes for every course of the curriculum, and a trilingual grade and GPA calculator used by KTÜ civil engineering students.

I completed an Erasmus+ internship at Universitatea Politehnica din Timișoara, Romania, and a site internship at Fen Mühendislik in Trabzon. I work comfortably in Arabic, Turkish, and English.

Projects

SHEET 2 OF 7
Graduation Thesis · 2026

Next-Generation Sustainable School Campus Design

Design of a next-generation sustainable vocational school campus for 400 students in Trabzon Değirmendere, combining structural design with sustainability. Structural side: full seismic analysis of a 4-story reinforced concrete building in SAP2000 to the Turkish Building Earthquake Code (TBDY-2018) — modal analysis, equivalent static vs. response spectrum base shear, story drift, and irregularity checks — plus campus modeling in ETABS and ideCAD. Sustainability side: solar PV, rainwater harvesting, passive insulation, and IoT-based monitoring. Completed with three other students under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Metin Hüsem.

Telegram Bot + Web Tool · 440+ Users

KTÜ İnşaat Botu (Civil Engineering Bot)

A Telegram bot serving KTÜ civil engineering students through a full button-based menu: pick a semester, pick a subject, and the bot instantly shares all the lecture notes and study materials for that course. It also links to a companion trilingual (Turkish / English / Arabic) web calculator that computes course grades and GPA, including a reverse-engineered implementation of KTÜ's T-score relative grading algorithm. The bot is complete and actively used by more than 440 students.
